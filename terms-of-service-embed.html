4. TERMS FOR JOB SEEKERS

4.1 Resume Submission and Processing

4.1.1 Voluntary Submission. Job Seekers may submit their resumes directly to the Company at any time through the designated submission form.

4.1.2 Review Process. After submission, the Company will review the resume and may contact the Job Seeker to confirm optional preferences regarding publication and distribution.

4.1.3 Three-Repository System. Upon acceptance, Job Seeker information will be processed and stored in three locations:

a. Raw Resume Repository: The original, unredacted resume will be saved to a secure, cloud-based file repository maintained by the Company. Access to Raw Resumes is restricted and controlled by the Company.

b. Redacted Resume Repository: To support Job Seeker privacy, the Company will create a redacted copy of the resume by removing or obscuring:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Social media links and handles
  • Any other personally identifiable contact information

The Redacted Resume will be stored in a separate cloud-based repository and made viewable to anyone accessing the Resume Page.

c. Metadata File: Non-personally identifiable information derived from the Redacted Resume (such as specialty, agencies supported, job functions, and experience categories) will be stored in a publicly accessible cloud-based metadata file used for UI sorting and filtering. This metadata file shall not include personal names or contact information.

4.2 Job Seeker Rights and Responsibilities

4.2.1 Accuracy. Job Seekers represent and warrant that all information provided in their resumes is truthful, accurate, and complete to the best of their knowledge.

4.2.2 Right to Update. Job Seekers may request updates to their resumes at any time by contacting the Company. The Company will make reasonable efforts to process updates in a timely manner.

4.2.3 Right to Remove. Job Seekers may request removal of their resumes from the Resume Repository at any time by submitting a written request via email to donald@donaldleads.com. The Company will remove the resume within 10 business days of receiving such written request.

4.2.4 Notification of Requests. When a Requestor requests an Unredacted Resume, the Company will copy (CC) the Job Seeker on the email distribution of their resume, providing the Job Seeker with visibility into who is requesting their information.

4.2.5 Direct Communication. Job Seekers acknowledge that once their Unredacted Resume is shared with a Requestor, the Requestor may contact them directly. Job Seekers are not obligated to respond to or engage with any Requestor.

5. TERMS FOR REQUESTORS

5.1 Eligibility and Certification

5.1.1 Authorized Purposes. Requestors may only request Unredacted Resumes if they are involved in one of the following authorized activities:

  • a. Direct Hiring: Recruiting, sourcing, or hiring for direct employment positions within their own organization
  • b. Referral Programs: Operating a legitimate employee or contractor referral program for their own organization

5.1.2 Prohibited Purposes. Requestors may NOT request Unredacted Resumes for the following purposes:

  • Third-party recruiting or staffing activities (unless acting as a direct hiring authority)
  • Building databases or contact lists for general distribution
  • Marketing, sales, or promotional purposes
  • Any purpose other than considering the Job Seeker for a bona fide direct hire or contract opportunity with the Requestor's organization

5.1.3 Certification of Intent. By submitting a request for an Unredacted Resume, Requestor certifies that:

  • They have a real or potential job opportunity for which they are considering the subject Job Seeker(s)
  • The potential job opportunity is more likely than not to materialize (i.e., greater than 50% probability)
  • The opportunity offers compensation at fair market rates for the position and geographic area
  • They intend to consider the subject Job Seeker(s) for a direct hire or direct contract role with their organization

5.1.4 Good Faith Engagement. Job Seekers make their resumes available through the Service with the understanding that Requestors are inquiring about resumes in connection with real or potential job opportunities meeting the criteria in Section 5.1.3. While Requestors are not bound to hire any Job Seeker whose resume was requested, all requests must be made in good faith.
